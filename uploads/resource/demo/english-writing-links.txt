高中英语作文连接词清单

1. 开头引入
- To begin with
- First of all
- As far as I am concerned

2. 递进补充
- Besides
- Furthermore
- In addition

3. 举例说明
- For example
- For instance
- Such as

4. 因果关系
- Because
- Therefore
- As a result

5. 总结收束
- In conclusion
- To sum up
- All in all

6. 扩写建议
- 先写主题句，再补理由
- 每段至少加入一个连接词
- 用 because, which, when 等结构扩充句子
